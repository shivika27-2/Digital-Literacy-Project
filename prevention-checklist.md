# 🛡️ Stay Safe Online — Prevention Checklist
### For College Students in India | Digital Ambassador Project

---

## 🔐 Account & Password Safety
- [ ] Use a strong password with letters, numbers, and symbols (e.g., R@ndom#2024)
- [ ] Never use the same password for multiple accounts (email, Instagram, banking)
- [ ] Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) on all important accounts
- [ ] Never share your OTP with anyone — banks and UPI apps NEVER ask for it

## 💸 Financial & UPI Safety
- [ ] Remember: You NEVER need to enter your UPI PIN to RECEIVE money
- [ ] Never accept "collect requests" from unknown contacts on PhonePe/GPay/Paytm
- [ ] Set a daily UPI transaction limit in your banking app to reduce fraud risk
- [ ] Verify the receiver's UPI ID carefully before every transaction

## 📱 Social Media & Personal Safety
- [ ] Keep your social media profiles private — avoid sharing your phone number publicly
- [ ] Never click unknown links received via WhatsApp, SMS, or Instagram DMs
- [ ] Do not post your college ID, Aadhaar card, or address on any platform

## 💻 Device & Network Safety
- [ ] Avoid using public Wi-Fi for banking, UPI, or logging into personal accounts
- [ ] Always lock your phone/laptop with a PIN, password, or fingerprint

---

## 🚨 Report Cybercrime Immediately
| Channel | Details |
|---|---|
| 🌐 Online Portal | cybercrime.gov.in |
| 📞 Helpline Number | **1930** (National Cyber Crime Helpline) |
| 🏛️ Local Police | File an FIR at your nearest cyber cell |

> **Remember:** Report within 24 hours for the best chance of recovering lost money.
